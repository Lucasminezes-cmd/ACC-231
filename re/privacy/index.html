Last updated: July 14, 2026

Stallion Health Journal ("SHJ", "we", "us", or "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

When you click those links, the third party may collect information about you under its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party sites.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable law requires it, we process personal data based on your consent, our legitimate interest in operating and improving the site, and compliance with legal obligations.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at editorial@stallionhealthjournal.digital.

Changes to This Policy
